Privacy Policy for LiveLAN

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Thank you for using LiveLAN.

This Privacy Policy explains how LiveLAN handles information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

LiveLAN does not collect, store, or sell personal information.

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Camera Permission

LiveLAN requires access to your device's camera to capture live video for streaming over your local network.

Camera data is processed on your device and used only for the streaming session you initiate.

Microphone Permission

LiveLAN may request access to your device's microphone if you choose to enable audio streaming.

Microphone access is optional and is used only while audio streaming is enabled by you.

Network Access

LiveLAN uses your device's network connection to stream video (and optional audio) to compatible devices on the same local network (LAN).

The app does not transmit your media to servers operated by the developer.

Data Collection

LiveLAN does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Analytics data
  • Crash reports

Data Sharing

LiveLAN does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

LiveLAN does not store your streamed video or audio by default.

Any media processed by the app is used only during the active streaming session.

Children's Privacy

LiveLAN is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date at the top of this document.
